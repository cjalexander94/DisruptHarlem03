Bruce Wayne



Bruce Wayne is a Gotham born and raised billionaire who fights crime at night dressed as Batman. He rarely but occasionally works along side the Justice league to save the world. He is known for his cunning nature and his detective skills. He once devised a plan that was stolen from him and led to the takedown of the entire justice league.



Batman is a pretty strong hero due to his intellect.



Batman often has trouble working with others because he is a loner and untrusting.
