Tony Stark



Tony Stark is a "billionaire, playboy and a philanthropist" who also uses his immense robits knowledge as well as his resources to save the world. He developed his own Iron Man suit which gives him abilities that surpass humans and even some superhumans. Tony Stark was the most important hero in saving the world when the Djitari attacked New York.



Tony Stark's greatest feats come from his generous heart.



Tony Stark is the best and most realistic of the Marvel superheroes.
