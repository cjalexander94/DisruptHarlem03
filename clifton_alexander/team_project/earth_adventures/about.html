Adventures in Learning

Earth Adventures lets you be the explorer. Start your journey at any point on the map and play games as you move through the different parts of our Earth and solar system. Stack up rewards and unlock cool experiments along the way!


We started Earth Adventures because we believe that everyone deserves to enjoy science and to learn about the world around them. And what better way to do that then by exploring our Earth? Whether you're a student, parent, or teacher, Earth Adventures has something for you.
