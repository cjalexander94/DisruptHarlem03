Parents Join Us!

Earth Adventures is an interactive learning and gaming site for kids but it doesn't just stop there. We need you, the parents, to join in the fun. We wish to not only make children better students but to open their minds and make them want to learn more about various topics related to science. The games on this site are presented in a story format where a child can follow a character around the Earth and beyond. Each level or location features different activities. Want to learn how you can join and what you can do? Look below!

You looked below! Good job! Just click sign up on the top right corner of the page. After you sign up and your child has, you can go to link accounts to connect both your accounts. You just type in your child's email and select their name. You can also connect with teachers if they have a account set up. If the teacher sets up a lesson plan, you can see your child's progress. You, a parent, can also set up your own lesson plan! Now that you are ready, enjoy! If you have any questions, please go to the FAQ page.
